Shine On Album

Shine On is an album that project a tribute to Mr Kofi ANNAN , a Ghanaian diplomat who served as the seventh Secretary -General of the United Nations from January 1997 to December 2006 .Mr Annan and the UN were the co-recipients of the 2001 Nobel Peace Prize . The second album by the Ivorian Guitarist /composer Constant Boty is produce by Mevicon Services and co-produced by Jacques Jolicoeur A.K.A ‘DJ Imhotep from Mauritius Island. The album features musicians from different parts of the world such Benito Gonzalez (Venezuela),Heather Maxwell (USA),Lonnie Plaxico (USA), Kris Allen (USA),Luis Santos(Brazil), Kobena (Ghana),Daniel Prim ,Oran Etkin (Israel),Nkumu Katalay (D.R.C Congo )Francisco Mela (Cuba)Maiko Hata (Japan),Daniel Freedman (Israel).
